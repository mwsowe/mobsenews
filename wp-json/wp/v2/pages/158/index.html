The scruture of the education system<\/h2>

The basic structure of the education system in The Gambia provides for the expansion of basic education (ECD, Basic Education 1 - 9 and Adult and Non-formal Education) and Secondary Education.\n\nThe formal system is marked by 9 years of continuous basic education followed by 3 years of senior secondary education and four years of post-secondary or tertiary and higher education.\n\nThe first 9 years of schooling make up the basic education route and is financed mainly by The Government while secondary school education, which lasts 3 years, is primarily provided by the private sector with grant-aided schools assisted by Government and managed by school boards.\n\nIn line with the Dakar Framework for Action on EFA, the basic education Programme is composed of the following areas:\n
\n\u2022 Early Child Care
\n\n\u2022 Basic Education (from Grades 1-9)
\n\n\u2022 Adult and Non-Formal Education
